Leh, Ladakh

Overview

Weather in Leh

Leh's weather is generally cold, with temperatures dropping further in the evenings and early mornings. Due to high UV levels, it’s essential to take sun protection measures when outdoors—use sunscreen with a high SPF and wear protective clothing during the afternoon. For the early mornings and evenings, layering up is advisable to keep warm. Always carry sufficient water to stay hydrated in the dry, high-altitude conditions.

Words of Advice

Traveling in high-altitude regions like Ladakh can make you prone to illness. Keep your emergency information updated and accessible. Share health insurance details with travel companions, or if solo, display emergency contacts and insurance info on your phone's lock screen.

Exploring Leh and Nearby Areas

Shanti stupa, Leh palace, Old castle (Tsemo hill) and Tsemo Maitreya temple, Leh market. All these places are close to Leh market, except Shanti stupa which is 3.4 km from Leh market.

Day Tour from Leh City


  • Leh market → Stok palace (15.2 km) → Shey palace (14.3 km) → Thiksey monastery (18.9 km) → Hemis monastery (36.1 km) → Chang La pass (76.1 km). The distances in parentheses are from Leh market.   • Leh market → Hall of fame, Leh (5.6 km) → Spituk monastery (8.1 km) → Gurudwara Pathir Sahib (24.6 km) → Magnetic hill (27.9 km) → Intersection of Srinagar-Leh Highway and Zanskar Valley Road → Indus and Zanskar river sangam (30.9 km) → Zanskar valley view point (57.5 km) → Likir Gompa → Alchi monastery → Lamayuru monastery. The distances in parentheses are from the Leh market. The route forks at the intersection of Leh-Srinagar highway and Zanskar valley road. Likir Gompa and Alchi monastery are at a little detour from the main path.

Multi-Day Tour from Leh City


  • Leh market → Khardung La pass (37.2 km) → Khalsar (92 km) → Diskit monastery (112 km) → Hunder (123 km) → Nubra valley (158 km). The distances in parentheses are from Leh market. Lamayuru Monastery

Shanti Stupa

There are two ways to enter Shanti stupa: by road (easy hike) and through stairs (easy to moderate hike).
There was an entrance fee of Rs. 30 for entering by road.
On the way to the stupa from the main road, you'll come across a meditation center that makes for a peaceful stop. The Shanti Stupa offers stunning views of Leh city and the surrounding mountain ranges. It is a good spot to see sunrise and sunset.

Leh Palace

Old Castle (Tsemo Hill)

There’s a shortcut from Leh Market to Leh Palace Road, but parts of the route are rocky and uneven. The path is unmarked, so it’s advisable to ask a local for directions. The hike from Leh Palace is moderately challenging.
The hike up to Old Castle Hill is free, but accessing the Tsemo Maitreya Temple at the summit requires an entrance fee of Rs. 20.
From the hilltop, you can enjoy breathtaking views of Leh city and the surrounding mountain ranges. It's also a good spot for watching both sunrise and sunset.

Leh Market

The Leh market features a range of shops to meet tourists' needs, including travel agencies, bike rentals, souvenir stores, restaurants, and grocery shops. Vegetables and fruits are available in the alleys next to the main market.
