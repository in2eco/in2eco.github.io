Rupay Cards

Overview

RuPay is an Indian domestic card scheme launched by the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI). It was developed to establish a secure, affordable, and accessible electronic payment system across India. RuPay cards are widely accepted across India and are increasingly being accepted internationally through partnerships with other global payment networks like Discover, JCB, and Diners Club. RuPay competes with global payment networks like Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. RuPay cards are typically more affordable for banks to issue, as they have lower transaction processing fees compared to other international card networks.
